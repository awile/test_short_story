# The Leak - Script

## Format Notes

- Vertical short-form drama
- Estimated runtime: 30-60 seconds
- Primary focus: Ethan and Marcus fighting over Richard's approval and the Vale family legacy

## Cast

- Ethan Vale: younger son, junior employee, presumed favorite
- Marcus Vale: older brother, colder and more controlled
- Richard Vale: their father, founder of Vale & Rowe
- Northstar Petroleum Voice: offscreen voicemail

## Scene 1 - Interior, Vale & Rowe Law Firm Office - Day

Ethan's phone lights up in his hand.

On screen:

```text
Dad: Come to my office. Now.
```

Ethan straightens his tie. He tries not to smile. This is the call he has been waiting for.

Across the hall, Marcus watches from the doorway. He already knows this is not a promotion.

Ethan steps into Richard's office.

**Ethan**  
You wanted to see me? Is this about the promotion?

Richard does not answer immediately. He closes the folder in front of him.

**Richard**  
Close the door.

Ethan's smile drops.

## Scene 2 - Interior, Richard's Office - Continuous

Richard slides a printed email across the desk. Ethan sees his own account name on it.

**Richard**  
The leak came from your account.

**Ethan**  
What leak?

Marcus stands beside Richard, not behind Ethan. The placement tells Ethan everything: Marcus has already been invited into the conversation.

Ethan's phone starts buzzing.

On screen:

```text
Northstar Petroleum stock plunges after leaked offshore liability memo
Regulators review Northstar drilling permits after disclosure
```

Richard looks furious, but underneath it he is afraid.

**Richard**  
Northstar Petroleum was this firm's first-ever client. They lost two billion before breakfast. You are done here.

**Ethan**  
Dad, I swear to you, I did not send anything.

**Marcus**  
For once, Ethan, do not make him clean up after you.

Ethan turns to Marcus, stung by the older-brother tone more than the accusation.

## Scene 3 - Interior, Conference Room - Moments Later

Ethan sits at the conference table. Richard stands at the head of it.

Marcus takes the chair Ethan expected to inherit.

**Ethan**  
Give me one hour. Let me pull the logs before you throw me out.

Richard looks at his younger son. For a second, he almost looks like a father instead of a managing partner.

**Richard**  
I think the Vale name survives only if you leave now.

Ethan looks from Richard to Marcus. Marcus does not smile, but he does not need to.

## Scene 4 - Interior, Parking Garage - Day

Ethan walks through the garage carrying a cardboard box of desk items.

Marcus follows him into the colder light.

**Ethan**  
You smug bastard. You leaked it.

Marcus drops the polished office mask.

**Marcus**  
I proved what everyone knew. You were never ready.

**Ethan**  
You burned my name so you could sit in Dad's chair.

**Marcus**  
I took back what was mine. Now move, Ethan. I have actual work to take care of.

Ethan's phone buzzes again.

On screen:

```text
Document accessed from Marcus Vale's terminal - 11:48 PM
```

Ethan stares at the proof.

A voicemail begins to play.

**Northstar Petroleum Voice**  
Your family is lying. Ethan was not the only one with access.

Ethan looks back toward the law firm tower.

**Ethan**  
Then this is not about clearing my name. It is about ending yours.

Marcus watches him, realizing Ethan is no longer asking to be let back in.

## End Hook

Richard asks Marcus to step into Ethan's place at the firm, giving Marcus exactly what he wanted. Ethan now has evidence his older brother framed him, but using it will force him to decide whether he wants his father's approval back or wants to burn down the lie that held the family together.
